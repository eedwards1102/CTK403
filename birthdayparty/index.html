What song was number one on my birthday?

The number one song we most likely hear on our birthday is "Happy Birthday."
But what song was the rest of the world hearing on the day I was born?

The Number One Song the Day I Joined the World

Credit to Genius(Opens in a new tab) for the image

According to Birthday Jams(Opens in a new tab), the number one song on my birthday was "Lose Yourself" by Eminem.

According to Billboard(Opens in a new tab), "Lose Yourself" was released in October 2002. The song shaped discussions about rap culture and was the first rap song to win an Academy Award for Best Original Song in 2003.
